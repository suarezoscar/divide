# Divide — Product Context

## Register
**Product surface** — app UI. The design helps someone finish a task quickly: split expenses and settle debts.

## Audience
Groups of friends, flatmates, and travel companions who share expenses and want to know who owes whom — without friction, without spreadsheets.

## Brand Voice
Warm, fast, discreet. Not corporate, not playful.

## Visual References
- Splitwise — group expense tracking, minimal UI
- SettleUp — debt minimization, clean mobile UX
- Glossar app — #07819C teal palette, Inter typography, rounded cards

## Anti-References
- Traditional banking apps (dense, intimidating)
- Corporate dashboards (complex, data-heavy)
- Anything that feels slow or bloated

## Key Design Principles
1. **Mobile-first** — most users manage expenses on their phone
2. **One-handed** — core actions (add expense, settle up) reachable with thumb
3. **Zero learning curve** — new users should join a group and add an expense in under 30 seconds
4. **Real-time** — changes from other group members appear instantly
5. **Trustworthy** — balances and debt calculations must feel precise and fair
